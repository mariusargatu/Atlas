# Router AX2 connectivity troubleshooting guide

Customers using the Router AX2 on the Fiber 100 Legacy plan should follow this
guide if connectivity issues occur.

Applies to Fiber 100 Legacy at 28.99 per month in the region-coast region.

Step 1: verify firmware version 1.4.2 is installed on the
Router AX2.
Step 2: disconnect power for at least 30 seconds, then reconnect.
Step 3: confirm the connection status indicator shows a stable signal.

Support cases referencing the Router AX2 should include the firmware version,
1.4.2, for faster resolution.
