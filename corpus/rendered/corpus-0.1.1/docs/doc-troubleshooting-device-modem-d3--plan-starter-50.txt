# Modem D3 connectivity troubleshooting guide

Customers using the Modem D3 on the Starter 50 plan should follow this
guide if connectivity issues occur.

Applies to Starter 50 at 19.99 per month.

Step 1: verify firmware version 3.2.1 is installed on the
Modem D3.
Step 2: disconnect power for at least 30 seconds, then reconnect.
Step 3: confirm the connection status indicator shows a stable signal.

Support cases referencing the Modem D3 should include the firmware version,
3.2.1, for faster resolution.
