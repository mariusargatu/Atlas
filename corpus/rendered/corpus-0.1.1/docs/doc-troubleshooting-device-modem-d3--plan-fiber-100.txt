# Troubleshooting: Modem D3

If your Modem D3 is not connecting properly on the Fiber 100 plan, try
these steps.

Applies to Fiber 100 at 29.99 per month.

1. Confirm the device firmware is at version 3.2.1 or later.
2. Power cycle the Modem D3 for 30 seconds, then reconnect.
3. Confirm the link light is solid before retrying your connection.

If the issue persists, contact support and reference your Modem D3 firmware
version 3.2.1.
