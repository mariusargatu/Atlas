# Router BE1 connectivity troubleshooting guide

Customers using the Router BE1 on the Fiber 500 plan should follow this
guide if connectivity issues occur.

Applies to Fiber 500 at 41.99 per month in the region-central region.

Step 1: verify firmware version 1.0.1 is installed on the
Router BE1.
Step 2: disconnect power for at least 30 seconds, then reconnect.
Step 3: confirm the connection status indicator shows a stable signal.

Support cases referencing the Router BE1 should include the firmware version,
1.0.1, for faster resolution.
