# Troubleshooting: Router AX2

If your Router AX2 is not connecting properly on the Fiber 100 plan, try
these steps.

Applies to Fiber 100 at 31.99 per month in the region-central region.

1. Confirm the device firmware is at version 1.4.2 or later.
2. Power cycle the Router AX2 for 30 seconds, then reconnect.
3. Confirm the link light is solid before retrying your connection.

If the issue persists, contact support and reference your Router AX2 firmware
version 1.4.2.
