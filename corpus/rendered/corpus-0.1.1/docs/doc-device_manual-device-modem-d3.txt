# Modem D3 device manual

Model: Modem D3
Device type: modem
Firmware version: 3.2.1

## Care and maintenance

Keep the Modem D3 in a well ventilated location away from direct sunlight.
Do not submerge the device in water.

## Firmware

This manual reflects firmware version 3.2.1. Check for
updates periodically to keep the Modem D3 compatible with future plans.
