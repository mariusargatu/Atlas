# Troubleshooting: Router BE1

If your Router BE1 is not connecting properly on the Fiber 500 plan, try
these steps.

Applies to Fiber 500 at 36.99 per month in the region-coast region.

1. Confirm the device firmware is at version 1.0.1 or later.
2. Power cycle the Router BE1 for 30 seconds, then reconnect.
3. Confirm the link light is solid before retrying your connection.

If the issue persists, contact support and reference your Router BE1 firmware
version 1.0.1.
