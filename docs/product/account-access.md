# Account and plan information

Customers can ask Atlas to look up their own account or our current plans. They get an answer without logging into the customer portal.

## What a customer can ask about their own account

1. A customer can ask about their current plan and whether it has a contract.
2. A customer can ask about their data use for the current billing period and their data cap if they have one.
3. A customer can ask about their current bill. This includes the amount, the due date and whether it has been paid.
4. A customer can ask about their equipment. This includes what is installed, the model and the serial number.
5. A customer can ask about their open support tickets. Closed tickets are not shown. A customer who wants their full ticket history needs to ask a person today.

## What a customer can ask about our plans

1. A customer can ask what plans we offer.
2. A customer can ask about a specific plan. This includes whether it has a contract and what the early exit fee is if it does.
3. A customer can ask the monthly price for a plan.
4. A customer can ask if they are eligible to move to a plan. Right now the only plans we block a move to are ones we have discontinued.

We kept two plans live in this phase. One is the plan we sell today. It has no contract. The other is the older plan some existing customers are still on. That older plan is where our real risk sits. A customer on it is under a contract our general help content never mentions, because that content only describes what we sell today. Any conversation that touches this older plan needs extra care.

## Known limitation

Account records sit in a temporary store for this phase. They reset between test runs. A permanent account store is planned for general availability. It is not built yet.
